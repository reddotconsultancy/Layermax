Message from Chairman & MD

Mr. Divesh Savla

Chairman & MD

Mr. Divesh Savla is the guiding force behind the success of our business venture. His sharp business acumen and unwavering ethics have always been a source of inspiration to our entire team. We are delighted to welcome you as one of our esteemed clients.

We specialize in all kinds of furniture — Home, Café, Outdoor, Educational, Office, Modular, and Hotel. From sleek modular setups to durable outdoor solutions, our diverse range caters to every space with comfort, quality, and elegance.

“Right furniture enhances space.” With this belief, Decorative was founded in 1995. Since then, we have grown into one of India’s leading furniture manufacturers.

We proudly serve a wide clientele, including furniture dealers, residences, offices, hotels, sanatoriums, educational institutions, and corporate establishments.

Premium designer chairs and comfort recliners crafted for contemporary homes and professional work environments.
