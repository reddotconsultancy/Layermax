Premium Delivery Services

We design comfort, and we ensure it is delivered with precision. Every Layermax product is transported under strict protection controls and assembled by certified technicians in your home or office space.

Free Standard Shipping

We offer free standard doorway shipping on all collections to major locations in South and Central India.

White-Glove Assembly

Our logistics team will carry the package inside, unbox it, inspect the build details, and assemble the furniture in your room of choice.

Secure Crate Protection

To ensure structural safety, heavy recliners and sofas are enclosed in thick foam padding and custom wood crates.

Delivery Timelines

Standard Chairs

Processed within 2 business days. Delivered in 5–7 business days post-dispatch.

Lounge & Recliners

Handcrafted to order. Built in 7–10 days. Total delivery time: 12–15 business days.

Custom Furniture

Custom lengths, widths, or specialized upholstery configurations require 15–20 days for hand fabrication. Total delivery time: 20–25 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I get a delivery coordination phone call?

Yes! 24–48 hours before delivery, our local warehouse manager will phone you to coordinate a convenient 3-hour delivery window.

What happens if my item is damaged in transit?

If our white-glove team discovers structural flaws or fabric damage upon setup, they will pack the product back up immediately, note it on the receipt, and trigger a replacement order instantly. If doorway delivery was chosen, contact Contact Us within 24 hours.
