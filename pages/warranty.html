Guarantee of Excellence

12-Month Manufacturing Warranty

We stand behind our materials and craftsmanship. Every Layermax product is backed by a 12-month warranty against manufacturing defects and component failures.

Warranty Coverage

  • Defects & Components: 12 months warranty covering manufacturing defects, faulty assembly, and faulty and/or missing components.
  • Exchange, Repair, or Refund: During the warranty period we undertake to exchange, repair, or refund the product after review, verification, and confirmation of the defect by our QC team.
  • Extension Limit: The length of the warranty for a repaired or replaced product is equal to the remaining length of the original product's warranty.

Natural Characteristics

  • Solid Wood Variations: Furniture made of solid wood or natural materials has distinct natural differences and occasional minor blemishes.
  • Not Treated as Defects: Inherent markings, texture variations, and minor blemishes are normal characteristics and not treated as defects under warranty.
  • Standard Exclusions: Damage from improper usage, chemical cleanups, accidental drops, cuts, and standard wear are excluded.

How to Submit a Warranty Claim

If you encounter structural framework concerns, please collect your details and contact our support team. We will send a service coordinator to your home for inspection and process a replacement or repair at our cost.

Step 1

Find Receipt

Locate your order receipt or invoice detailing the purchase date.

Step 2

Take Photos

Take clear photos showing the frame joint or weld issue from multiple angles.

Step 3

File Request

Submit details via our Contact Us page.
